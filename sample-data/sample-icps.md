# Sample ICPs for Testing

## B2B SaaS Sales Tool
```
Find VP of Sales or Head of Revenue at B2B SaaS companies
with 50-200 employees in the US who recently raised Series A-B funding
```

## AI/ML Consulting
```
Find CTO or VP of Engineering at technology companies with 100-500 employees
in US/EU who are building AI/ML products
```

## Patent/IP Services (Inverse ICP)
```
Find leads for a patent attorney specializing in AI/ML, VR/AR, and NLP.
Target: Founders and CTOs at AI startups with 10-100 employees in Seattle
who are building proprietary technology at Seed to Series B stage.
```

## HR Tech Platform
```
Find Head of People or VP of HR at fast-growing startups with 200-1000
employees in North America who are scaling their hiring
```

## Cybersecurity
```
Find CISO or VP of Security at FinTech companies with 100-500 employees
in the US who handle sensitive financial data
```
